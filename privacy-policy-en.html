Your Privacy Matters

At HeySage, we understand that mental health conversations are deeply personal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our AI mental health assistant service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Apple Sign In, we receive your Apple ID and optionally your name and email address
  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to our AI assistant, including text content and conversation history
  • Profile Information: Any personal information you choose to share during conversations
  • Subscription Information: Payment details processed through Apple's App Store (we don't store payment information directly)

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features you use, session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage patterns to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide AI Assistance: Process your messages to generate helpful, personalized responses
  • Improve Service: Analyze conversation patterns to enhance our AI's capabilities
  • Personalization: Tailor responses and recommendations based on your conversation history
  • Account Management: Manage your subscription and provide customer support

2.2 Secondary Uses

  • Safety Monitoring: Detect potential self-harm or crisis situations (automated only)
  • Technical Maintenance: Debug issues, prevent fraud, ensure security
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • Research: Conduct anonymized research to improve mental health AI (opt-in only)

3. Data Protection & Security

3.1 Encryption

  • All conversations are encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are managed separately from encrypted data

3.2 Access Controls

  • Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • All access is logged and monitored
  • Staff undergo background checks and privacy training

3.3 Data Minimization

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for our service
  • Automatic deletion of old conversation data (configurable by user)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers or analytics companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Third-party AI training datasets

4.2 Limited Sharing (Only When Necessary):

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our service (under strict contracts)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Emergency Situations: If we believe immediate action is needed to prevent harm
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with user notification)

5. Your Rights & Controls

5.1 Access & Portability

  • View all your personal data we've collected
  • Export your conversation history in a readable format
  • Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format

5.2 Correction & Deletion

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete specific conversations or your entire account
  • Request complete data deletion (with 30-day verification period)

5.3 Control & Preferences

  • Opt out of optional data collection
  • Set automatic conversation deletion timeframes
  • Control what data is used for service improvement
  • Manage notification and communication preferences

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Additional safeguards for sensitive mental health data
  • Regular compliance audits of international operations

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

  • Conversation Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Account Information: Kept while your account is active plus 30 days
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 2 years
  • Legal Data: Retained as long as legally required

7.2 Automatic Deletion Options

  • Set conversations to auto-delete after 30, 90, or 365 days
  • Immediate deletion available for sensitive conversations
  • Complete account deletion removes all data within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

HeySage is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

For Users 13-17:

  • Parental consent may be required in some jurisdictions
  • Enhanced privacy protections apply
  • Limited data collection and retention
  • Special crisis intervention protocols

⚠️ Mental Health Emergency Disclaimer

Important: HeySage is not a substitute for professional medical care or emergency services. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm, or suicidal ideation, please contact emergency services immediately or reach out to a crisis hotline in your area.

Crisis Resources:

  • Emergency Services: Call 911 (US), 999 (UK), or your local emergency number
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 (US)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Find local resources

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify you of material changes through the app or email
  • Provide 30 days notice before changes take effect
  • Highlight what has changed in our update notifications
  • Maintain previous versions for your reference
