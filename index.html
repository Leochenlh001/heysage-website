Who We Help

HeySage is designed for anyone seeking emotional support and mental wellness guidance:

  • Young adults navigating life transitions and career pressures
  • Individuals dealing with anxiety, stress, and daily emotional challenges
  • People in underserved areas with limited access to mental health professionals
  • Those seeking immediate support during difficult moments
  • Anyone wanting to build better emotional resilience and coping skills

Our Mission

We're breaking down barriers to mental health support by creating an AI assistant that combines the accessibility of technology with the warmth and expertise of professional psychological care. Our goal is to democratize mental health support, making it available to everyone who needs it, when they need it.

How

HeySage combines cutting-edge AI technology with evidence-based psychological principles to deliver professional-grade mental health support that's both accessible and effective.

  • Continuous learning from therapeutic best practices and user feedback

Research

HeySage is committed to advancing the field of AI-powered mental health support through rigorous research and evidence-based development. We believe that effective mental health technology must be grounded in scientific validation and continuous improvement.

Our research initiatives focus on developing and validating AI interventions that demonstrably improve mental health outcomes. We are actively establishing partnerships with leading academic institutions and research centers to conduct clinical studies, analyze real-world effectiveness data, and contribute to the growing body of knowledge in digital mental health.

Research Areas

  • Efficacy validation of AI-delivered CBT interventions
  • Personalization algorithms for mental health support
  • Cross-cultural adaptation of psychological interventions
  • Privacy-preserving methods for mental health data analysis
  • Long-term impact assessment of AI mental health tools

We welcome collaboration opportunities with researchers, clinicians, and institutions who share our commitment to evidence-based mental health innovation. Whether you're interested in clinical trials, outcome studies, or methodological research, we'd love to explore how we can work together.
